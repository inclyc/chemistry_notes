\chapter{固液界面}

\section{液体对固体的润湿}

主要有\{粘附,浸渍,铺展\}润湿。

\begin{enumerate}
    \item 粘附润湿 $W_a = \left(\gamma_{sg} + \gamma_{lg}\right) - \gamma_{s/l} \geq 0$
    \item 浸渍润湿 $W_i = \gamma_{sg} - \gamma_{sl} \geq 0$
    \item 铺展润湿 $S = S_{sg} - \left(\gamma_{sl} + \gamma_{lg}\right) \geq 0$
\end{enumerate}
